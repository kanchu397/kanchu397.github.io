Methodology & Skills

The Goal: The objective of these projects was to move beyond just analyzing past data and into the realm of forecasting future performance to make a concrete investment recommendation. This is a core skill for any equity research or corporate finance role.

The Process: For each company, I followed a rigorous process:

  • Analyzed historical financial statements (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow).
  • Forecasted future free cash flows based on revenue growth assumptions, margins, and capital expenditures.
  • Calculated the WACC to use as the discount rate.
  • Calculated the terminal value using the Gordon Growth Model.
  • Conducted sensitivity analysis to see how the final valuation changed based on different assumptions for growth and WACC.

These projects solidified my expertise in Advanced Excel, financial modeling, and fundamental investment analysis.
