My Journey

From Crisis to Clarity

My path to becoming an analyst wasn't traditional. After my MBA, a family emergency called me home to run our family business. For over seven months, theory became practice. I managed operations, handled client relationships, and reconciled finances daily.

It was during this time, faced with a mountain of disorganized data, that I had my 'aha' moment. I realized my true passion was finding the story hidden in the numbers. I taught myself what I needed, used Python to consolidate our records, and built a BI dashboard that changed how we saw our own business. That experience was my real-world MBA, and it's the resilient, problem-solving mindset I bring to every challenge.
